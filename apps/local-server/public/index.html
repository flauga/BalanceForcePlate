CSV format

timestamp, elapsed_s, fl_g, fr_g, bl_g, br_g, total_g, cop_x_mm, cop_y_mm

Calibration method

Per-cell calibration: zero load (offset) + one or two known weights placed directly over each corner. Scale factor = raw_tared / known_g. A second weight verifies linearity — if the two scale factors differ by >5% the firmware warns of cell nonlinearity. All values persist to EEPROM and survive power cycles. EEPROM is wiped on every flash.
